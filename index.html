For the International Children's Games 2015 are held in Alkmaar, The Netherlands. We have build a custom application to register all 3000 event visitors, participants and VIP's. The application is used by an audience from all over the world. More then 75 cities will be participating in the Games.

The output from the App is used by the organisation for a wide range of purposes. For example for planning logistics, catering, maps, competition schedules, security plans and additional events.
